<Grid>
  <SpaceProblem>
    ### Objective
    Motivate candidates to apply for vacancies on the site.
    
    Facilitate contact with the recruiter through the site and its form.
  </SpaceProblem>

  <SpaceProblem>
    ### User
    Tech professionals (Programmers, Designers, Data Analysts), Mid-Seniors and Seniors who are looking for a new and better job.
  </SpaceProblem>

  <SpaceProblem>
    ### User Needs
    Make it easier for candidates to find the best vacancies.
    
    Find support and advice from other community members.
  </SpaceProblem>

  <SpaceProblem>
    ### Context
    
    They use their free time or days off to look for vacancies.
    Time spent looking vacancies is limited.
  </SpaceProblem>
</Grid>

<Comment>
  By understanding the problem space, I identified the essential processes. This helped me figure out the navigation, categories and content structure.
</Comment>

<Image src="projects/lrd-redesign/lrd-redesign-content-structure" alt="Estructura de contenido propuesta para el proyecto." className="full-width" width="1000"/>

<Comment> 
  I did “Tree Testing” and “Card Sorting” tests for validate the labeling and ways of categorizing the content so that they would be clear to users.
  
  Once the content structure was validated, I started the sketching and interface design stage. Thanks to the sketches and previous information, I got a general idea of the components and the hierarchy I needed to build.
</Comment>
