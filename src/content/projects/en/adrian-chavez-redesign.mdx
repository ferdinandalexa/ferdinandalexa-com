<Comment>
 In a first analysis, the main problems I detected were accessibility, design consistency, and a difficult navigation between pages.

  I built a content inventory. This helped me to sort and categorize the website information to then work on the design of the componentes and differents ways to navigate into this information.
</Comment>

</Comment>
